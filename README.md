## Music-Energy-Rating-Project

## Overview 
A project In PowerBI That revolved around the danceability and energy of songs and rated them by their combined score.
I got the data from Spotify Songs of 2024 in which shows the high popularity in songs and their energy and danceability, which I did add together to get a answer of what was the most popular song and genre.

## Motivation/Main Question
For motivation, I am a guitar player and I listen to alot of music and I wanted to see which genre of music and specific songs you could dance to

### Technologies used
- **POWERBI** (data analysis and visualization)
